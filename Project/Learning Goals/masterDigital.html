Your Desired Roadmap

Must Do's

  • Create a Portfolio: Show digital marketing campaigns you've worked on.
  • Learn Google Analytics: Get certified in Google Analytics and Adwords.
  • Master Social Media: Understand how to leverage platforms like Facebook, Instagram, and LinkedIn for marketing.
  • Build SEO Skills: Learn SEO techniques to drive traffic to websites.

Steps You Can Follow

  • Master Marketing Tools: Become proficient with tools like Hootsuite, Google Analytics, and HubSpot.
  • Focus on Content Marketing: Learn how to create compelling content that drives conversions.
  • Learn Paid Advertising: Master Google Ads and Facebook Ads to reach your target audience.

Resources For You

  • HubSpot: Resources for digital marketing and sales.
  • Coursera: Courses on digital marketing, SEO, and content creation.
  • Udemy: Learn about social media marketing and Google Ads.

Example Roles For You

  • Digital Marketing Manager
  • SEO Specialist
  • Social Media Manager
