Your Desired Roadmap

Must Do's

  • Master Musical Scales: Learn major and minor scales and modes to understand musical structure.
  • Study Harmony: Develop skills in harmony, chord progressions, and voice leading.
  • Analyze Compositions: Break down musical compositions to understand form, structure, and style.
  • Practice Ear Training: Develop your ear for identifying intervals, chords, and rhythms.

Steps You Can Follow

  • Focus on Counterpoint: Learn how to write music with multiple independent lines.
  • Study Orchestration: Explore how to arrange music for different ensembles and instruments.
  • Master Composition Techniques: Create original compositions in various styles and genres.

Resources For You

  • MusicTheory.net: Lessons and exercises in music theory.
  • Coursera: Courses on music theory and composition.
  • Berklee Online: Learn from world-renowned music educators.

Example Roles For You

  • Music Composer
  • Music Theorist
  • Orchestrator

Recommended Books For You

  • "The Complete Musician" by Steven G. Laitz
  • "Tonal Harmony" by Stefan Kostka
  • "Counterpoint" by Johann Fux
