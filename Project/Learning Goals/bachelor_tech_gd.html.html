Your Desired Roadmap

Must Do's

  • Build a Portfolio:

    Create a strong portfolio to showcase your design skills.

  • Learn Adobe Creative Suite:

    Master tools like Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign.

  • Get Involved in Design Communities:

    Join online forums, attend design meetups, and participate in design challenges.

  • Understand the Basics of UI/UX:

    Learn how to create user-friendly designs for digital products.

Steps You Can Follow

  • Master Typography:

    Learn the principles of typography and how to use fonts effectively.

  • Practice Branding:

    Understand how to create visual identities for businesses.

  • Study Color Theory:

    Learn how to use colors to evoke emotions and create visual harmony.

  • Work on Real Projects:

    Gain experience by working on real-world design projects, internships, or freelance work.

Resources For You

  • Behance: Showcase and discover creative work.
  • Lynda: Tutorials on graphic design tools and techniques.
  • Skillshare: Online classes in graphic design and illustration.
