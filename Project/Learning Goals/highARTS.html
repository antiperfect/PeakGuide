Your Desired Roadmap

Must Do's

  • Visit Museums & Galleries:

    Regularly explore art collections to deepen your understanding of different movements and styles.

  • Research Major Movements:

    Study Renaissance, Baroque, Modernism, and Postmodernism art to understand the progression of artistic expression.

  • Engage in Art Criticism:

    Practice writing art critiques and engage with contemporary art discourse.

Steps You Can Follow

  • Develop Research Skills:

    Conduct deep research on specific art periods, artists, or cultural influences.

  • Attend Art History Seminars:

    Participate in academic discussions and conferences on art history.

  • Work on Field Projects:

    Get involved in curating exhibitions, publishing papers, or interning at galleries.

Resources For You

  • Khan Academy - Art history courses and materials.
  • Smarthistory - Multimedia educational materials for art history.

Career Possibilities

  • Art Historian
  • Museum Curator
  • Art Critic

Recommended Books For You

  • "The Story of Art" by E.H. Gombrich: A classic introduction to the history of art.
  • "Ways of Seeing" by John Berger: A look into how we perceive art and images in contemporary society.
