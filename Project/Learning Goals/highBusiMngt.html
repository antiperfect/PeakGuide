Your Desired Roadmap

Must Do's

  • Start a Blog or Vlog on Business Topics:

    Share your insights on business trends, management strategies, or entrepreneurial ideas. Build a personal brand.

  • Participate in Business Competitions:

    Look for local or national competitions such as business pitch contests or case study challenges.

  • Join a Business Club:

    Get involved in school or community business clubs to build your network and gain leadership skills.

  • Explore Part-Time Work:

    Find part-time roles in small businesses to gain hands-on experience in management, sales, and marketing.

Steps You Can Follow

  • Take Relevant High School Courses:

    Enroll in economics, marketing, accounting, and business classes at your high school.

  • Engage with Online Business Communities:

    Join forums or social media groups where business topics are discussed to stay updated on trends.

  • Shadow Business Leaders:

    Seek opportunities to shadow or intern with local business owners or managers to see how business functions in real life.

  • Study Basic Financial Literacy:

    Develop an understanding of personal finance, investing, and managing small budgets.

Resources For You

  • Khan Academy - Free business and economics lessons.
  • FutureLearn - Online courses in business management for beginners.
  • TED Talks - Inspirational talks on leadership, innovation, and business strategies.

Career Possibilities

  • Entrepreneur
  • Business Consultant

Recommended Books For You

  • "Rich Dad Poor Dad" by Robert Kiyosaki: Learn the importance of financial literacy.
  • "The 7 Habits of Highly Effective People" by Stephen Covey: Develop leadership and personal management skills.
  • "The Art of the Start 2.0" by Guy Kawasaki: A guide to starting your own business or project.
