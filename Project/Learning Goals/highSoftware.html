Your Desired Roadmap

Must Do's

  • Create a Portfolio:

    Showcase your projects and research. Keep updating them as you advance in your master's studies.

  • Engage in Networking:

    Connect with industry professionals and academic leaders through platforms like LinkedIn.

  • Participate in Conferences:

    Attend industry-specific conferences and seminars to stay ahead of trends and build your network.

  • Work on Internships and Projects:

    Engage in internships or real-world projects related to your field of study.

Steps You Can Follow

  • Stay Updated with Trends:

    Regularly follow trends in your field, whether it's Graphic Design, Psychology, Business Management, etc.

  • Join Study Groups:

    Form study groups with peers to collaborate on difficult topics.

  • Seek Mentorship:

    Find mentors in your chosen field who can guide you through academic and professional challenges.

Resources For You

  • Behance (Graphic Design inspiration and portfolios)
  • Coursera (Courses on Machine Learning, Psychology, Business Management)
  • MoMA (Art History and Resources)
  • LinkedIn Learning (Courses in business, technology, and creative skills)

Recommended Books For You

  • "Thinking with Type" by Ellen Lupton: A critical guide to typography for Graphic Designers.
  • "The Body Keeps the Score" by Bessel van der Kolk: A must-read for students in Psychology.
  • "The Innovator's Dilemma" by Clayton M. Christensen: Insights into Business Management and innovation.
  • "Deep Learning" by Ian Goodfellow: A foundational book on Machine Learning.
  • "Ways of Seeing" by John Berger: A classic for Art History students.
  • "The Rest Is Noise" by Alex Ross: Essential reading for Music Theory enthusiasts.
  • "An Actor Prepares" by Konstantin Stanislavski: A must for aspiring actors.
