Your Desired Roadmap

Must Do's

  • Attend Leadership Workshops: Develop leadership skills for managing teams.
  • Learn Financial Management: Understand how to manage and allocate resources effectively.
  • Network with Business Professionals: Build connections that can lead to career opportunities.

Steps You Can Follow

  • Focus on Project Management: Learn how to plan, execute, and close projects successfully.
  • Understand Business Operations: Study how businesses function and how to improve efficiency.
  • Master Strategic Planning: Develop long-term strategies to achieve business goals.

Example Roles For You

  • Business Manager
  • Project Manager
  • Operations Manager

Recommended Books For You

  • "The Lean Startup" by Eric Ries: Learn efficient business management principles.
  • "Good to Great" by Jim Collins: Understand what makes businesses achieve greatness.
  • "Blue Ocean Strategy" by W. Chan Kim: Learn how to create new market space and avoid competition.
