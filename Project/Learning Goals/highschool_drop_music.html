Your Desired Roadmap

Must Do's

  • Learn Basic Music Notation: Understand how to read and write sheet music.
  • Study Scales and Chords: Explore different scales, intervals, and chord progressions.
  • Analyze Songs: Break down popular songs to understand their musical structure.

Steps You Can Follow

  • Practice with Instruments: Apply your knowledge of music theory to the piano, guitar, or another instrument.
  • Write Your Own Music: Use your understanding of theory to compose original pieces.
  • Collaborate with Musicians: Work with others to improve your skills and gain experience.

Example Roles For You

  • Music Producer

Recommended Books For You
