Your Desired Roadmap

Must Do's

  • Create a Portfolio: Showcase your design work on platforms like Behance and Dribbble.
  • Learn Design Tools: Master tools like Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and Figma.
  • Join Design Communities: Engage with design communities like Awwwards and UX Design Slack groups.
  • Build a Personal Brand: Establish your online presence as a designer.

Steps You Can Follow

  • Master Design Tools: Learn Adobe Creative Cloud, Figma, and other design software.
  • Focus on Typography and Layout: Understand the fundamentals of typography, color theory, and composition.
  • Learn UX/UI Design: Get familiar with user-centered design principles and design thinking methodologies.

Resources For You

  • Skillshare: Design tutorials and courses.
  • Udemy: Learn design software and UI/UX principles.
  • LinkedIn Learning: Graphic design courses and tutorials.

Example Roles For You

  • Graphic Designer
  • UX/UI Designer
  • Product Designer

Recommended Books For You

  • "The Elements of User Experience" by Jesse James Garrett
  • "Don't Make Me Think" by Steve Krug
  • "Thinking with Type" by Ellen Lupton
