Your Desired Roadmap

Must Do's

  • Join Psychology Club:

    Participate in discussions, workshops, and activities related to psychology.

  • Explore Online Resources:

    Read articles and watch videos on basic psychological concepts and theories.

  • Volunteer for Research Projects:

    Seek opportunities to assist in psychology research at local universities or labs.

  • Attend Psychology Workshops and Seminars:

    Engage with professionals and gain insights into the field.

Steps You Can Follow

  • Take Advanced Psychology Courses:

    Enroll in AP Psychology or similar courses to deepen your knowledge.

  • Participate in Psychology-related Extracurriculars:

    Join related clubs or groups that offer practical experience and networking.

  • Consider Summer Internships:

    Look for internships in mental health clinics or research labs.

Resources For You

  • Simply Psychology Articles and resources on psychological theories and concepts.
  • Psychology Today Insights and articles on mental health and psychology.
  • Coursera Online courses in psychology and mental health.

Recommended Books For You

  • "Psychology: Themes and Variations" by Wayne Weiten: A comprehensive introduction to psychology.
  • "Thinking, Fast and Slow" by Daniel Kahneman: Explore cognitive psychology and decision-making.
  • "The Man Who Mistook His Wife for a Hat" by Oliver Sacks: Fascinating case studies in neurology.
