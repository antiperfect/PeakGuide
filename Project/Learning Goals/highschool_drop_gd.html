Your Desired Roadmap

Must Do's

  • Learn Design Basics: Study color theory, typography, and layout principles.
  • Master Design Software: Learn tools like Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and Figma.
  • Create a Portfolio: Build a strong portfolio showcasing your design projects.

Steps You Can Follow

  • Practice Daily: Create designs regularly to improve your skills.
  • Freelance Projects: Take on small freelance gigs to build experience and network.
  • Keep Learning: Stay updated with the latest design trends and software updates.
