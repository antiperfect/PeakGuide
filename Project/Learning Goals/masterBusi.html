Your Desired Roadmap

Must Do's

  • Develop Leadership Skills: Practice team leadership and management in various scenarios.
  • Understand Business Strategy: Learn how to build and implement strategic business plans.
  • Master Financial Analysis: Understand financial statements, balance sheets, and budget forecasting.
  • Build Networking Skills: Connect with industry professionals to build relationships and opportunities.

Steps You Can Follow

  • Study Business Models: Learn various models like SWOT and PESTEL for strategic planning.
  • Improve Communication Skills: Focus on verbal and written communication in business contexts.
  • Master Project Management: Learn project management tools like Trello, Jira, and Asana.

Resources For You

  • Harvard Business Review: Insightful articles on business strategies and leadership.
  • Coursera: Courses on business management and leadership.
  • Udemy: Learn business strategies and financial analysis.

Example Roles For You

  • Business Analyst
  • Project Manager
  • Operations Manager

Recommended Books For You

  • "Good to Great" by Jim Collins
  • "The Lean Startup" by Eric Ries
  • "Leaders Eat Last" by Simon Sinek
