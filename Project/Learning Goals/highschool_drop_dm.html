Your Desired Roadmap

Must Do's

  • Learn SEO Basics: Study how search engines rank content and optimize your projects accordingly.
  • Social Media Marketing: Understand how to leverage platforms like Facebook, Instagram, and LinkedIn for brand promotion.
  • Email Marketing: Learn how to create engaging email campaigns using tools like Mailchimp or HubSpot.

Steps You Can Follow

  • Run Small Campaigns: Use Google Ads or Facebook Ads to promote a product and analyze the results.
  • Master Analytics: Learn Google Analytics to track traffic and measure the effectiveness of marketing campaigns.
  • Content Strategy: Create blog posts, infographics, and videos that align with business goals.
