Must Do's

  • Create LinkedIn Account:

    Regularly post insights from your research, business strategies, and case studies. Connect with industry leaders, alumni, and professionals in business and finance.

  • Create Twitter (X) Account:

    Share industry analyses, business trends, and your own research findings. Engage in conversations with business experts and follow key thought leaders.

  • Build a Strong GitHub Portfolio:

    For business analytics, include advanced projects such as predictive models, market analysis reports, or financial forecasting. Showcase any tools or applications you've developed.

  • Attend Webinars and Business Conferences:

    Participate in high-level events focused on business strategy, financial technology, and market trends to stay updated and network with key professionals.

Steps You Can Follow

  • Apply For Mid-Level Positions

    Consider roles such as Business Consultant, Financial Analyst, or Strategic Planner. Tailor your applications to emphasize your advanced analytical skills and strategic thinking.

  • Build a Strong Resume:

    Highlight advanced projects, research papers, and relevant internships. Focus on leadership roles, strategic impact, and quantitative skills.

  • Earn advanced Certifications:

    Obtain certifications in areas like Business Analytics (e.g., CBAP, CBA), Project Management (e.g., PMP), or Financial Analysis (e.g., CFA Level 1 or 2).
