    Share your artwork, creative processes, and engage with the art community. Follow and connect with artists who focus on health-related themes.

    If relevant, showcase digital art projects or interactive installations that incorporate medical themes or data visualization.

  • Attend Art Exhibitions and Workshops:

    Participate in art events that focus on themes of health and medicine, or those that allow you to showcase your medical background in your art.

    Include exhibitions, art projects with health themes, and any related internships. Highlight skills in art software, multimedia projects, and thematic expertise.

  • Earn advanced Certifications:

    Consider certifications in medical illustration, digital art, or art therapy.
