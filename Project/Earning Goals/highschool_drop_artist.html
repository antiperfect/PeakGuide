Your Desired Roadmap

Career Pathways Exploration

  1. Freelance Artist: Work as an independent artist creating commissioned art.
  2. Graphic Designer: Design visuals for websites, brands, or media.
  3. Illustrator: Create illustrations for books, magazines, or online content.
  4. Tattoo Artist: Learn and practice the art of tattooing.

Skill Development

  1. Drawing Fundamentals: Learn the basics of drawing, color theory, and shading.
  2. Digital Art: Practice using graphic design software such as Adobe Illustrator or Procreate.
  3. Portfolio Building: Start building a portfolio to showcase your work.
  4. Art Mediums: Experiment with different art mediums such as watercolor, acrylics, or ink.

Resources For You

  1. Online Art Courses: Platforms like Skillshare and Udemy offer art courses.
  2. Art Communities: Join local or online art communities to share your work.
  3. Design Software: Learn to use design software like Adobe Photoshop or GIMP.

Example Roles

  • Freelance Artist: Sell or create commissioned art.
  • Illustrator: Design illustrations for various media.
  • Graphic Designer: Work with brands to design visuals.

Roadmap For Advancement

  • Short-Term Courses: Enroll in design or illustration courses.
  • Certifications: Earn certifications in graphic design or digital art.
  • Portfolio Development: Continue to build and refine your portfolio.
