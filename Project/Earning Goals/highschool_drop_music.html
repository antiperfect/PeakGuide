Your Desired Roadmap

Career Pathways Exploration

  1. Freelance Musician: Perform in local venues or online as a freelance musician.
  2. Music Teacher: Teach music lessons or tutor students in instruments.
  3. Music Producer: Get involved in recording and producing music tracks.
  4. Sound Technician: Work in sound mixing and technical setup for events or studios.

Skill Development

  1. Music Theory Basics: Learn the basics of music theory and composition.
  2. Instrument Proficiency: Practice and become proficient in at least one instrument.
  3. Audio Production Skills: Learn how to use audio production software.
  4. Performance Skills: Gain experience performing live in front of audiences.

Resources For You

  1. Online Music Lessons: Platforms like YouTube or Udemy provide free music lessons.
  2. Community Classes: Enroll in local music classes or workshops.
  3. Recording Software: Learn how to use software like GarageBand or Ableton Live.

Example Roles

  • Freelance Musician: Perform at local gigs or events.
  • Music Tutor: Teach music lessons.
  • Producer: Produce tracks for yourself or other artists.

Roadmap For Advancement

  • Short-Term Courses: Learn more about sound production and instrument mastery.
  • Certifications: Earn certifications in audio engineering or sound mixing.
  • Performance Experience: Attend or organize open-mic events.
