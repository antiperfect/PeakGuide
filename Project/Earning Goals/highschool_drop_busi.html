Career Pathways Exploration

  1. Entrepreneurship: Start your own business by identifying local market opportunities.
  2. Freelance Business Services: Offer freelance services like marketing or customer support.
  3. Small Business Management: Work in management positions in SMEs.
  4. Retail or Sales: Explore careers in retail management or sales.
  5. Online Business: Use e-commerce platforms to sell products online.

Skill Development

  1. Basic Financial Literacy: Learn to manage finances like budgeting and investing.
  2. Sales & Marketing: Develop sales strategies and marketing techniques.
  3. Customer Service: Build strong communication and relationship skills.
  4. Entrepreneurial Mindset: Cultivate problem-solving and adaptability skills.

Resources For You

  1. Online Courses: Use platforms like Coursera or Udemy for business courses.
  2. Business Mentorship: Seek advice from business mentors.
  3. Government Programs: Explore support programs for entrepreneurs.
  4. E-commerce Platforms: Use Shopify or Etsy to start an online business.

Example Roles

  • Entrepreneur: Start your own business or side hustle.
  • Retail Manager: Manage a small retail store.
  • Freelancer: Offer business services.

Roadmap For Advancement

  • Short-Term Courses: Take short business courses.
  • Certifications: Earn certifications in marketing or finance.
  • Networking: Attend local business networking events.
