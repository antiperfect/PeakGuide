Your Desired Roadmap

Must Do's

  • Create LinkedIn Account:

    Highlight your advanced business skills, strategic insights, and leadership experience. Share articles, case studies, and thought leadership pieces related to business strategy, management, and innovation.

  • Create Twitter (X) Account:

    Engage with industry leaders, share your views on business trends, and participate in discussions about market strategies and corporate management.

  • Build a Strong GitHub Portfolio:

    Create a professional website or portfolio showcasing your strategic projects, case studies, and consulting work. Include testimonials and detailed descriptions of your impact in previous roles.

  • Attend Industry Conferences and Workshops:

    Participate in high-profile business conferences, workshops, and webinars to stay updated on the latest trends and network with key industry figures.

Steps You Can Follow

  • Apply For Senior Level Positions

    Target roles such as Business Development Manager, Strategy Consultant, or Senior Manager. Tailor your resume to highlight leadership experiences, strategic projects, and measurable impacts.

  • Build your Resume:

    Emphasize achievements in project management, strategic planning, and business transformation. Include any notable successes and quantifiable results.

  • Earn Relevant Certifications:

    Consider certifications in project management (e.g., PMP), strategic management (e.g., SBM), or advanced financial management.
